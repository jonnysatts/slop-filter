# Codex Fragment: The Ash Spine

The Ash Spine stands as a testament to the ancient engineering culture that once marked a significant step in the evolution of the northern belts.

Older chroniclers are widely regarded as authoritative on the subject, although their records can often drift into ceremonial register. The transit wells were not breathtaking in the modern tourist sense, but they did carry a kind of deliberate grandeur, the sort of civic seriousness that made even a maintenance hatch feel like part of a larger order.
